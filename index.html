About Us

The project is designed to teach beginners how to properly use cryptographic methods and easily apply them in practice to encrypt messages, media files, and regular files or archives. Our goal is to empower individuals to share their encrypted data with friends and live securely.

It is important to note that this project has exclusively positive intentions. We strongly oppose the use of our project for "hacker" or similar malicious purposes. We do not endorse illegal activities. Our aim is to show how cryptographic methods work in practice, and we provide users the opportunity to experiment with different data protection techniques on their own.

Our products offer free cryptography that is easy to master. Even someone with no programming experience can use it with the principle "download, run, and it works." However, anyone interested can always review the source code, conduct their own analysis, or customize the project to better fit their needs by modifying its components.
